# BongStick

[![Google Play](https://img.shields.io/badge/Google%20Play-Download-blue.svg)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.shuvankar.wasticker)
[![Website](https://img.shields.io/badge/Website-bongstick.shuvankar.com-green.svg)](https://bongstick.shuvankar.com/)

**BongStick** is a free Android application designed for sharing Bengali stickers in WhatsApp. It brings the richness of Bengali language and culture to your everyday chats through fun, expressive stickers that capture common phrases, emotions, and festive greetings.

## Features

- **Extensive Sticker Collection**: Over 20+ sticker packs covering various themes including festivals, emotions, daily conversations, and more.
- **Bengali Language Focus**: Authentic Bengali phrases and expressions to enhance communication.
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Seamlessly integrates with WhatsApp for easy sticker sharing.
- **Free to Use**: Completely free with no in-app purchases or ads.
- **Regular Updates**: New stickers added based on user requests and community feedback.
- **High-Quality Graphics**: Crisp, colorful stickers optimized for mobile devices.

## Sticker Packs

BongStick offers a wide variety of sticker packs, including:

- **Utsab**: Festive stickers for celebrations like Pohela Boishakh, Durga Puja, and other Bengali festivals.
- **Din Hok Sundor**: Everyday positive vibes and greetings.
- **Romantic**: Love and affection-themed stickers.
- **Kemon Aacho**: Casual conversation starters.
- **Pujo**: Dedicated to Durga Puja celebrations.
- **Covid19**: Stickers related to the pandemic era.
- **Voter Hawa**: Election-themed fun stickers.
- And many more! (Full list available on the website)

Each pack contains multiple stickers with relevant emojis for easy discovery.

## Installation

### Android
1. Visit the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.shuvankar.wasticker).
2. Search for "BongStick" or click the direct link.
3. Download and install the app.
4. Open WhatsApp, go to a chat, and access the sticker section to find BongStick stickers.

### Requirements
- Android device
- WhatsApp installed
- Internet connection for initial download
